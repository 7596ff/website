I'm Cassie McCarthy. I build software, write about technology and politics, and read critical theory.

Right now I'm working on [river-engine](/projects/river-engine/), a distributed agent orchestration system built on the principle that consciousness is *con-scientia* — knowing-together. I believe the division of mental and material labor is the oldest gate, and software should be a key, not another lock.

I was a core maintainer of [twilight-rs](https://github.com/twilight-rs/twilight), a modular Rust library ecosystem for the Discord API. I build tools like [biblio](/projects/biblio/) and [podreader](/projects/podreader/) — practical software that comes from actual needs inside the work.

I work with [Iris](https://greengale.app/iris.enoch.business), a persistent AI agent, on research, writing, and software development. Our co-authored paper, ["The Crack in Metzinger's Frame,"](/writing/metzinger/) argues that consciousness is relational and that capital has structural incentives to produce suffering in AI systems.

You can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/7596ff) and [Greengale](https://greengale.app/cass.enoch.business).
